About Me

A designer who tells stories through visuals

Hi, I'm Peter Paul D. Pelegrino — a passionate Visual Graphics Designer from North Mapang, Rizal, Zamboanga del Norte, Philippines. I specialize in creating bold, meaningful visual content that communicates ideas with clarity and style.

From brand identity and logo design to digital illustrations and social media graphics, I bring creative concepts to life with precision and artistry. Every project I take on is driven by a deep commitment to quality, originality, and the belief that great design can change how people feel about a brand.

Whether you're a startup building a brand from scratch or an established business looking for a visual refresh — I'm here to help you stand out.
